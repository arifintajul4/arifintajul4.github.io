WHAT IS STARFUND?

StarFund provides a platform for startups and companies to raise capital and financing via an integrated medium to facilitate flow of information among issuers, investors, lead and angel investors, venture capitalists and the likes of sophisticated investors.


StarFund helps investors to obtain thorough and real time updates on the progress of companies invested, and ability to sell their ownership to other investors via secondary trading platform, even while the company is still private. We believe that the blockchain based infrastructure of StarFund platform will provide a trustworthy, integrated and seamless flow of information without the need of disconnected process and system; hence streamlining the fund raising process and facilitating the secondary trading capabilities.


Until recently, access to pre-IPO companies has been limited. By leveraging technology and market expertise, our platform provides accredited investors access to pre-IPO, late stage technology investments at reasonable minimums via our platform.
